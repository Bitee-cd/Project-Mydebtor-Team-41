The Debt Validation Service for Schools

Studebt is built to help schools around Lagos list debtors, find debtors, clear debts

Who our services are for...

Schools

Schools in Lagos who register on the platform can make a list of school debtors, find information about a certain debtor or remove information once the debt has been cleared,

Parents and Students

Although parents or students are not permitted to list or remove information from the platform, they're allowed to file a complaint if they find inaccurate information on StuDebt regarding them or their loved ones.

Our services to explore

Filling you in on what Studebt is about & how we can help you build a solid foundation for your school.

Find school debtor information

Ever been in a situation where you are about to enrol a new student into your school but you aren't sure about their debt status in their previous school? With Studebt, you can find out their debt status.

List Student Debtors

Did a studebt of your school leave without clearing their fees, listing them on Studebt will ensure they don’t go to another school without clearing their debts.

Clear student debtors

As an authorized admin user of Studebt, you have the permission to clear the information of a studebt once it's certain their debt has been cleared.

Contend or File Complaints

Parents, students or a third party user are allowed to file a complaint on the platform regarding any inaccurate information they observe.
